Champions TT

Champions TT is a fan made Triple Triad game, based on the one introduced in Final Fantasy VIII, and building on the world of League of Legends.

Features

  • Sign up for free and receive 15 cards from the basic champion set and access to all emblems.
  • Construct a deck of five cards and an emblem to do battle on the Triple Triad board.
  • Challenge Aldous the AI, engage with other players, or invite a friend to play.

Roadmap

  • Unlockable champion cards and emblems to utilize the full set of League of Legends champions and skins.
  • Expand ruleset to include all existing Triple Triad conditions, and additional custom mechanics.
  • Provide daily and weekly missions to encourage players to try new challenges.
  • Raise your rating system through continued success in battle.

Requirements

A recent version of Chrome is recommended for the best experience at this time.
